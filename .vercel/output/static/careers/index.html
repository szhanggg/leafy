Leafy Culture

Leafy is a team that works together to produce creative, high-quality designs. Our team members are diligent and efficient, offering the best service to our customers. We have a work-hard, play-hard mentality and celebrate each success on our team. We put our all into each project, because that is what our customers deserve.

What we value

Here at Leafy, we value collaboration and consultation. We believe the best ideas come from everyone’s ideas mixed together. We are open to new suggestions and requests from anyone on our team. We encourage curiosity and asking questions, and we never back down from challenges. Every day working at Leafy is a new adventure.

Beliefs

Innovation

Innovation is the key to success. We love coming up with new ideas and unique plant designs as leaders in the industry.

Collaboration

Design is not an individual job. It requires multiple layers of ideas and input to create the perfect product. No part of our work flow is done alone.

Fulfillment

We make a promise to our customers—to put our all into outputting a high-quality product that satisfies their desires—and we fulfill that promise.

Benefits

We love our employees, and we only want the best for them. Employee benefits include:

Health Insurance

We value the health of our employees above anything. We offer health insurance plans with a variety of coverages to meet the needs of you and your family.

Unlimited PTO

Here at Leafy, we work hard and play harder. Our employees have unlimited paid-time-off to enjoy their lives outside of work, and come back to work even stronger.

401(k) Retirement Plan

We offer full contribution matching of our employee’s 401(k) plan.

Paid Family Leave

We understand that family comes first. We offer our employees paid family leave of up to 12 weeks to gradually transition into their new lives.

Remote-Friendly

We do not believe contributions should be limited geographically. We offer positions that are either partially or fully remote so you can be a part of the Leafy team from anywhere.

Flexible Schedule

Our managers are flexible with employee schedules so you can work according to your daily needs.
